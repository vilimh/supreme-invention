ME ME ME

When my inspiration is gone I just try to look at myself and I get inspired instantly... People often ask me, how can you be so briliant? And I say, Well it is cause im a GRAPHIC DESIGNER and all of the graphic designers are instantly cool and hipsters. In my line of work I think im the best, i make the most original work out there and alot of people are jealous of me but, i cant blame them it really is hard to be me.

I have been awarded much awards for my brilliant work. Alot of my friends dont get my art and they say ii is bad, well I guess you are not WOKE enough. Sometimes I cry myself to sleep because of my work. IT IS SO BRILLIANT. When I dont have a mirror by my side I often find other inspiration on tumblr.
